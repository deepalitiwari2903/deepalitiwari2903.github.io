02 Case Study — Margin Growth on a $1.2M/Month PPC Book Employer anonymized

US-based subscription Q&A platform · Customer Acquisition · Oct 2025 – Feb 2026

The Challenge

Inherit a mature, high-spend customer-acquisition portfolio across Google & Microsoft Ads — 800+ campaigns, 12 accounts, 2 MCCs, $1.2M+/month — and unlock further margin without inflating CPA. The portfolio had been optimized by multiple senior practitioners before me, so the easy levers were already pulled.

What I Did

  • Ran a portfolio-wide audit using SQL on SSMS against a 9M+ row database to identify structural drag — primarily ad groups carrying >50 keywords that were diluting Quality Score signals.
  • Designed and shipped a complex account restructure: completed the STA→RSA migration cleanup, then re-themed bloated ad groups into tighter intent clusters.
  • Built an LLM-assisted keyword-scrubbing workflow on Gemini Pro to categorize 2–3K Search Query Report (SQR) terms per cycle into branded / non-branded / medication names / non-English / geo terms — collapsing a 2–3 day manual task into 2–3 hours (~90% time saved).
  • Launched Google AI Max on pilot campaigns and validated lift via pre-vs-post comparison.
  • Continuous A/B experiments on bidding, ad copy, and audience expansion.
